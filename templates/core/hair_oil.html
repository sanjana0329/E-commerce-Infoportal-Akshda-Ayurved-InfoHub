Your Guide to Radiant Hair

Comprehensive Guide - Using, Storing, Timing.

Here is a comprehensive guide on using, storing, and timing the application of Nourishing Ayurvedic Hair Oil to maximize its benefits

How to Use

1 Warm the Oil:

Warm a small amount of oil by placing it in a bowl of warm water for a few minutes.

2 Massage the Scalp:

Gently massage the oil into the scalp using circular motions, focusing on the roots.

3 Apply to Hair Length:

Work the oil through the length of your hair, ensuring even coverage.

4 Allow Absorption Time:

Leave the oil on for at least 1-2 hours, or overnight for deeper nourishment.

5 Rinse with Mild Shampoo:

Wash thoroughly with a mild, natural shampoo.

When to Use

• Evening Application for Overnight Nourishment: For the best results, apply the oil in the evening before bedtime. This allows the oil to nourish and repair your hair overnight.

• Pre-Wash Treatment: Alternatively, apply the oil at least 1-2 hours before washing your hair for a quicker treatment.

• Avoid Application Before Outdoor Exposure: Avoid applying oil just before going out, as exposure to dust and pollution can affect the benefits.

How to store

Cool, Dark Place Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
Tightly Sealed Keep the container tightly sealed to preserve the oil’s potency.
Shake well before use Shake well before each use to ensure all ingredients are evenly distributed.
