Comprehensive Guide - Using, Storing, Timing.

Here is a comprehensive guide on using, storing, and timing the application of Kumkumadi oil to maximize its benefits

How to Use

1 Cleanse:

Start by cleansing your face to remove any dirt, oil, or makeup.

2 Application:

Take 2-3 drops of Kumkumadi oil on your fingertips. Gently warm it between your fingers.

3 Massage:

Apply the oil evenly across your face and neck. Gently massage in upward circular motions for 3-5 minutes, focusing on areas with pigmentation or dullness.

4 Absorption:

Leave the oil on for 20-30 minutes, then rinse with lukewarm water or leave it overnight for deeper absorption if your skin is comfortable with it.

When to Use

• At Night: Kumkumadi oil is best applied at night, as it allows the skin to absorb the nutrients without exposure to sun and environmental pollutants.

• Frequency: For dry or normal skin, use it every night. For sensitive or oily skin, start by using it 2-3 times a week and gradually increase as needed.

• Avoid Direct Sun Exposure: Since Kumkumadi oil contains ingredients like saffron and turmeric, which may increase sensitivity to sunlight, it’s best to avoid direct sun exposure immediately after applying.

How to store

Cool, Dark Place Store Kumkumadi oil in a cool, dark place, away from direct sunlight and heat, as this can degrade the natural ingredients.
Tightly Sealed Ensure the cap is tightly closed after each use to prevent oxidation.
Shelf Life Check the expiration date, and use within that timeframe for maximum efficacy.
Following these guidelines will help ensure that your Kumkumadi oil remains potent and effective, providing optimal results with regular use.
