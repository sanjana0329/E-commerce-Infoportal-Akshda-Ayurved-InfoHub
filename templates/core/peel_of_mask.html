Your Guide to Radiant Skin

Comprehensive Guide - Using, Storing, Timing.

Here is a comprehensive guide on using, storing, and timing the application of Brightening Peel-Off Mask to maximize its benefits

How to Use

1 Cleanse:

Start by cleansing your face with a gentle face wash to remove any dirt and makeup.

2 Application:

Apply an even layer of the peel-off mask to your face, avoiding the eyes, eyebrows, and lips.

Make sure the layer is thick enough for easy peeling.

3 Relax:

Allow the mask to dry completely. This usually takes about 20-30 minutes.

4 Peel Off:

Gently peel the mask starting from the edges and moving towards the center.

5 Aftercare:

Rinse off any residue with lukewarm water and follow up with a toner and moisturizer.

When to Use

• Use 1-2 Times Weekly: Use 1-2 times a week for best results.

• Evening Application for Best Results: Ideal to apply in the evening after your skin has been exposed to daily stressors like pollution or sunlight.

• Avoid Sun Exposure After Use: Avoid using right before going out in the sun as Vitamin C can make your skin more sensitive to sunlight.

How to store

Store in a Cool, Dry Place Store the product in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
Keep Container Tightly Closed Keep the container tightly closed to prevent the mask from drying out.
Avoid High Humidity Areas A bathroom cabinet is fine, but avoid areas with high humidity to maintain the product’s effectiveness.
