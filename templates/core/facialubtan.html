Your Guide to Radiant Skin

Comprehensive Guide - Using, Storing, Timing.

Here is a comprehensive guide on using, storing, and timing the application of Facial Ubtan to maximize its benefits

How to Use

1 Prepare the Ubtan Paste:

Take a small amount of ubtan powder and mix it with water, rosewater, milk, or yogurt to create a smooth paste.

2 Apply to Damp Skin:

Gently apply the paste to damp skin, covering the face and body if desired.

3 Exfoliate with Gentle Massage:

Massage in gentle, circular motions for a few minutes to exfoliate and boost circulation.

4 Allow Nourishment Time:

Leave the ubtan on for 5-10 minutes, allowing the ingredients to deeply nourish and purify the skin.

5 Rinse and Pat Dry:

Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat dry.

When to Use

• Use 2-3 Times Weekly: For best results, apply the ubtan 2-3 times a week to maintain healthy and glowing skin.

• Morning for Refreshing Start: Use the ubtan in the morning to refresh and rejuvenate your skin, leaving it soft and radiant for the day ahead.

• Evening for Deep Cleansing: Apply the ubtan in the evening to deeply cleanse your skin, removing impurities and leaving it nourished and purified.

How to store

Avoid Direct Sunlight and Heat Place the container in a cool, dry area away from sunlight and heat to preserve its freshness.
Store in an Airtight Container Keep the ubtan in a tightly sealed container to protect it from air and moisture.
Seal After Each Use Always close the container securely after use to prevent exposure to humidity and maintain its effectiveness.
Following these guidelines will ensure that your facial ubtan stays fresh and effective, delivering optimal results with regular use.
