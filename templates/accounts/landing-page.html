Sandalwood

Chandan, or sandalwood, is a revered natural ingredient known for its soothing, anti-inflammatory, and skin-brightening properties. It deeply nourishes, reduces acne, evens skin tone, and imparts a natural glow.

Artemisia Pallens (दवणा)

Artemisia Pallens, also known as Davana, is a fragrant herb known for its calming properties. It soothes skin, balances oil, reduces blemishes, and provides a refreshing, natural glow to the skin.

Kapur Kachri

Kapur Kachri, or Spiked Ginger Lily, is a powerful natural ingredient prized for promoting hair growth, reducing dandruff, and strengthening roots. It adds volume, shine, and health to hair naturally.

jatamansi

Jatamansi, or Indian Valerian, is a revered herb known for its calming, anti-inflammatory, and skin-brightening properties. It deeply nourishes, reduces acne, evens skin tone, and imparts a natural glow.

Patchouli

Patchouli is an aromatic herb known for its rich, earthy fragrance with hints of sweetness and spice. Widely used in perfumes, incense, and essential oils, it’s popular for its grounding, calming qualities.

Tulsi

Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is a revered herb in Ayurvedic medicine, celebrated for its medicinal properties. Known for boosting immunity and relieving stress, it offers a peppery, aromatic flavor in teas and remedies.

Haldi

Haldi, or turmeric, is a golden-yellow spice prized in cosmetics for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It brightens skin, reduces blemishes, and promotes a natural glow, enhancing overall skin health.

Kesar

Kesar, or saffron, is a luxurious spice known for its vibrant color and delicate aroma. Rich in antioxidants, it’s used in culinary and beauty applications, enhancing flavors, radiance, and skin tone.

Swet Chandan

Swet Chandan, or white sandalwood, is cherished in cosmetics for its soothing, aromatic qualities. It gently brightens skin, reduces blemishes, and provides a cooling effect, promoting a radiant, balanced complexion.

Rakt Chandan

Rakt Chandan, or red sandalwood, is valued in cosmetics for its healing and skin-enhancing properties. It reduces pigmentation, soothes inflammation, and imparts a natural glow, making skin look healthy and rejuvenated.

Lodhra(Symplocos)

Symplocos, known as Lodhra, is a traditional herb in cosmetics celebrated for its skin-firming and clarifying effects. It reduces acne, tightens pores, and promotes an even-toned, radiant complexion naturally.

Agaru(Aquilaria malaccensis)

Aquilaria malaccensis, or agaru, is prized in cosmetics for its rich, woody fragrance and skin-nourishing properties. It hydrates, revitalizes skin, and offers a soothing effect, enhancing overall beauty and radiance.

sugandhi valo

Sugandhi valo, or aromatic vetiver, is cherished in cosmetics for its calming scent and skin-nourishing properties. It hydrates, balances oily skin, and promotes relaxation, enhancing overall skin health and well-being.

Liquorice(Jethimadh)

Liquorice, known for its sweet flavor, is valued in cosmetics for its skin-brightening and soothing properties. It helps reduce dark spots, evens skin tone, and provides anti-inflammatory benefits for sensitive skin.

Taj Patra

Taj Patra, or bay leaf, is valued in cosmetics for its antioxidant and antibacterial properties. It helps purify skin, reduce blemishes, and soothe irritation, promoting a clear and healthy complexion.
